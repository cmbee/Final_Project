Who We Are



Founded in the begining of 2019,
Youth Is The Future offers a safe creative space for at risk and troubled youth to creativly explore.

Community Growth

By engaging families and members of the community through programs, performances, and events held within our creative space.3

Mentorship Program

Mentorship programs offered by some of the industrys leading proffesionals. Providing youth hands on look and experience in their furute carrer field

Creative problem solving

Assisting both the youth and their families with creatively solving problems or issues. This is a key skill needed to becoming an asset to a company or industry.

Professional teaching artists in well-equipped studios, provide hands-on instruction in a range of subject areas within the visual, performing and new media arts. Intern and mentorship programs are provided to the youth the jumpstart their potential carrers as a creative. We also support community growth by engaging families and members of the community through programs, performances, and events hosted in our creative space. We are dedicated to bringing high-quality arts education to students of all ages and backgrounds. We offer these services all year round for free to our youth in underserved highcrime areas to mitigate some of the "at-risk" factors that negatively impact their lives. We embrace the opportunity to “give back” and provide the youth with services that we were not able to afford or obtain in our youth. Our program uses art to create an atmosphere of support where instruction in art and mentoring in social skills can take place. Youth Is The Future currently serves some 150 children mainly in the age range of 4-18 from the 5 boroughs of NewYork.
